Common Household Pets

Canis lupus familiaris
Domestic dog - a highly variable mammal closely related to the gray wolf.
Felis silvestris catus
Domestic cat - a small, predatory feline mammal.
Oryctolagus cuniculus domesticus
Domestic rabbit - a small plant-eating mammal with long ears, long hind legs, and a short tail.
Cavia porcellus
Domestic guinea pig - a small stout-bodied short-eared tailless domesticated rodent of South American origin.
